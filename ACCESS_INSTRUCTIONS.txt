ClaimIQ — Access Instructions (for reviewers)

ClaimIQ is a live, email-driven agentic claims pipeline. No reviewer account, install, API key, or setup is required for any path below.

START HERE — WEBSITE
Open: https://nikhil0075.github.io/ClaimIQ_AI_Website/#access
The Access section links every entry point and includes an on-page playground that runs the scenarios instantly (fully offline, zero setup).

RUN A REAL CLAIM BY EMAIL (recommended)
The pipeline reads a live inbox and processes any claim emailed to it.
1. Send your claim email to: claim.iq.ai.001@gmail.com
2. Fastest way to build the email: use a ready-made bundle. In the website's
   "Test-data claim bundles — one folder per scenario" section, each scenario
   (Motor, Health Emergency, ACL, Property Fire, Travel) gives you the exact
   claim email body and its supporting documents. Copy the email text as your
   message body, attach that folder's files, and send to the address above.
3. Within ~1-2 minutes you'll receive an automated acknowledgment, then
   mid-pipeline status updates, and a final adjuster-brief email with a PDF
   guide and a Google Drive link to all documents.

LIVE STREAMLIT DASHBOARD (no email needed)
https://claimiappihackathon-0075.streamlit.app/
Runs the same 7-stage pipeline with a full explainability view: per-claim
agent outputs, fraud signal breakdown, triage colour/SLA, and the adjuster
brief. If the app is asleep, click "Yes, get this app back up" and wait ~30s.

TEST DATA / ATTACHMENTS
Every claim email body and attachment can be downloaded from the
"Test-data claim bundles — one folder per scenario" section on the website
(each scenario also links its documents in a shared Google Drive folder).
